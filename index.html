WE BELIEVE IN

ColorCode is a student initiative founded out of Columbia's Intercultural Resources Center.
We support tech projects by and for underrepresented people of color.

GET IN TOUCH

This is what we're about


We're not your usual #diversity incubator. Recognizing that the tech industry routinely exploits communities of color while excluding people of color from its ranks, ColorCode aims to support tech projects built by and for people of color. We invest our time, energy, and resources into initiatives that dismantle existing systems of oppression rather than reinforcing them. Our work revolves around three core tenets.


Mentorship

Connecting underrepresented people of color to the people, institutions, and resources they need to get things built.

Skill Building

Empowering underrepresented people of color to build technology for themselves and their own communities.

Advocacy

Using our platform to demand greater access for underrepresented people of color--first at Columbia University, then at other institutions as well.
